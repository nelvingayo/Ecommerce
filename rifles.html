Navy Arms Winchester

If you've ever watched Chuck Connors in "The Rifleman" or seen a John Wayne western movie, odds are, you've seen a Model 1892. Elevated to star status by many of the most famous on-screen cowboys over a 75 year period, the Model 1892 is one of the few American firearms to have over a million produced. Enormously popular, compact, light and fast, the Model 1892 was an improved version of its predecessor, the Model 1886. Scaled down and simplified for smaller modern calibers, this nostalgic rifle still shines. Navy Arms produces one of the finest reproduction 1892 rifles available. This rifle features a bone charcoal color case hardened receiver and a 20" octagonal barrel.
